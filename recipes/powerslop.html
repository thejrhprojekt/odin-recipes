Powerslop

Years ago, a man named Jared was looking for a nutritional solution to his busy schedule. He didn't have time to cook, so he had to come up with a relatively quick meal that was nutritious, not too disgusting, and met his macronutrient needs. What he came up with would change the world forever when frickin' Huel pretty much stole his idea and made like a trillion dollars with it. It's best not to look at powerslop. So here's a puppy.

What You'll Need

  • 1 cup of quick cooking brown rice
  • 1 can of pinto beans
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/2 a cup of plain greek yogurt
  • Taco seasoning
  • Ton of hot sauce

Directions

  1. Cook the brown rice according to package's directions.
  2. Mix in the beans and seasoning.
  3. Wait to cool a bit then mix in greek yogurt, to with avocado, and serve with hot sauce.
